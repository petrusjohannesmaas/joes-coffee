## Recommended Free Sources

- **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/coffee-roastery
- **Pexels**: https://pexels.com/search/coffee%20roastery/
- **UI Faces** (for headshots): https://uifaces.co/

## Tips

- Prefer landscape images for hero/feature shots (min 1600×900px)
- Portrait aspect ratio (3:4) works best for `founder-joe.jpg`
- Headshots should be square or 1:1 (they render as circles — 200×200px minimum)
- Compress images with https://squoosh.app before adding to the project
